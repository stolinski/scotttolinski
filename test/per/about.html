The mission:

Perpetua is a clothing boutique specializing in sustainable, environmentally friendly, organic fashion for women and men. Located in downtown Ann Arbor, we offer quality clothing, accessories, and a rotating stock of curiosities meant to diminish or reverse our negative impact on, and increase our appreciation of Earth. We believe that consumers needn’t sacrifice aesthetics in order to create a healthier planet.

In addition to providing a local source for responsible attire, Perpetua aims to be a contribution to the locality, and to the causes we believe in. Besides selling really cool, eco-friendly garb, we are here to promote and participate in the local culture that makes Ann Arbor the best city in the world.

About us:

The owners, Catheryn and John Snyder, often disagree in matters of taste, yet are masters in compromise, humor, and mutual education. Therefore, expect eclectic style, impeccable quality, open ears and minds, and an observably evolving venue for the sale of wares, discussion of ideas, and hatching of plans, both noble and dubious.
